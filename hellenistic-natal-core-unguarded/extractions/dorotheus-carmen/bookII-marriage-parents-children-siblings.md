# Dorotheus Extraction — Book II Marriage, Children, Aspects, And Placement Clusters

Source: Dorotheus of Sidon, *Carmen Astrologicum: The 'Umar al-Tabari Translation*, trans. Benjamin Dykes, 2017, Book II, printed pp. 118-175.

Transmission note: source is Dorotheus through 'Umar al-Tabari, translated by Dykes. This file paraphrases rule logic and avoids direct cookbook import.

Brennan cross-ref: Ch.9 aspects/configurations, Ch.10 places, Ch.13 topic rulers, Ch.14 benefic/malefic testimony, Ch.16 Lots.

## Method Takeaway

Book II is the heaviest cookbook-risk section in Dorotheus. The extraction rule is:

```text
do not import "planet X in place Y means Z"
extract the condition logic: topic significator + triplicity lords + Lot + place + aspect family + benefic/malefic mitigation
```

## Rule Card II.1 — Marriage Uses Venus, Venus Triplicity Lords, Seventh Place, And Lot

Source: `[Dorotheus *Carmen* II.1.1-16 via 'Umar al-Tabari → Dykes 2017, printed pp. 118-121]` `[cross-ref: Brennan Ch.13]`

Use when:

- reading marriage or partnership in a Dorothean natal topic frame.

Required data:

- Venus sign/place/condition;
- Venus triplicity lords by sect;
- seventh place and ruler;
- Lot of wedding/marriage if formula is verified;
- benefic/malefic testimony to Venus and seventh.

Procedure:

1. Start with Venus as natural significator for marriage.
2. Audit Venus's triplicity lords as support or damage to the topic.
3. Audit the seventh place and its ruler.
4. Add Lot of wedding/marriage as a derived cross-check.
5. Compare benefic and malefic testimony; do not judge from Venus alone.

Guardrail:

Do not treat marriage as guaranteed from one favorable Venus condition or denied from one malefic contact.

Destination:

- `rules/significator-hierarchy.md`
- `rules/lots-and-fortune.md`

## Rule Card II.2 — Wedding / Marriage Lots Are Topic-Specific Witnesses

Source: `[Dorotheus *Carmen* II.2.1-3, II.3.1-22, II.4.1-36, II.6.1-8 via 'Umar al-Tabari → Dykes 2017, printed pp. 121-132]` `[new to Dorotheus]` `[needs verification]`

Use when:

- a marriage reading has exact birth time and verified Dorothean Lot formulas.

Required data:

- source formula used;
- Lot sign/degree;
- Lot ruler;
- planets in aspect or co-presence with Lot;
- Venus and seventh-place stack.

Procedure:

1. Record the exact formula and whether it is day/night or gendered in the source.
2. Audit Lot sign, place, ruler, and witnesses.
3. Compare Lot testimony with Venus and seventh-place testimony.
4. Treat repeated testimony as stronger than any single Lot condition.
5. Preserve uncertainty when formulas vary or the text is gender-specific.

Guardrail:

Do not add all marriage Lots to the main skill without a formula table and transmission notes.

Destination:

- `rules/lots-and-fortune.md`

## Rule Card II.5 — Children Use Jupiter Triplicity Lords, Fifth-Topic Frame, And Lot Of Children

Source: `[Dorotheus *Carmen* II.8.1-4, II.9.1-17, II.10.1-14 via 'Umar al-Tabari → Dykes 2017, printed pp. 135-138]` `[new to Dorotheus]`

Use when:

- reading children or fertility as a natal topic.

Required data:

- fifth place and ruler;
- Jupiter and Jupiter triplicity lords;
- Lot of children and ruler;
- benefic/malefic testimony;
- sign fertility/sterility categories if used.

Procedure:

1. Start with the topic house and its ruler.
2. Add Jupiter and the triplicity lords of Jupiter as natural/topic support.
3. Add Lot of children only with formula and exact position recorded.
4. Use benefics/malefics to assess support or constraint.
5. Keep number/sex-counting methods separate from qualitative topic reading.

Guardrail:

Do not make concrete fertility or child-count claims in the general skill.

Destination:

- `rules/significator-hierarchy.md`
- `rules/lots-and-fortune.md`

## Rule Card II.8 — Aspect Family Has Distinct Strength And Tone

Source: `[Dorotheus *Carmen* II.14.1-23, II.15.1-36, II.16.1-33, II.17.1 via 'Umar al-Tabari → Dykes 2017, printed pp. 141-155]` `[cross-ref: Brennan Ch.9]`

Use when:

- judging configured planets in testimony maps.

Required data:

- sign-based aspect family;
- which planet is in superior/right/left position where relevant;
- benefic/malefic identity;
- sect and condition;
- reception or host relation if present.

Procedure:

1. Identify the sign-based aspect family first.
2. Treat trine as more supportive and sextile as similar but weaker.
3. Treat square and opposition as harsher, requiring mitigation.
4. Preserve directionality/overcoming where Dorotheus distinguishes looking-down or right/left squares.
5. Apply benefic/malefic condition and reception before final judgment.

Guardrail:

Do not rewrite aspect family from degree proximity if signs do not support it.

Destination:

- `rules/aspect-reception-rules.md`

## Rule Card II.9 — Benefic Mitigation And Malefic Corruption Are Recurrent Modifiers

Source: `[Dorotheus *Carmen* II.14-II.17 via 'Umar al-Tabari → Dykes 2017, printed pp. 141-155]` `[cross-ref: Brennan Ch.14]`

Use when:

- a testimony pair has mixed benefic/malefic involvement.

Required data:

- configured benefics and malefics;
- whether they see the topic significator, Lot, or Lot ruler;
- condition of the benefic/malefic.

Procedure:

1. Check whether benefics witness a malefic-heavy configuration.
2. Check whether malefics witness or dominate a normally favorable configuration.
3. Treat mitigation/corruption as modifying quality, not erasing the base testimony.
4. Report "mixed" when each side has independent support.

Guardrail:

Do not average mixed testimony into bland neutrality.

Destination:

- `rules/planet-condition-audit.md`
- `rules/aspect-reception-rules.md`

## Rule Card II.10 — Co-Presence Blends Natural Significations Through Condition

Source: `[Dorotheus *Carmen* II.18.1-9, II.19.1-9, II.20.1-9, II.21.1-2, II.22.1-3, II.23.1 via 'Umar al-Tabari → Dykes 2017, printed pp. 156-160]` `[new to Dorotheus]`

Use when:

- two planets are co-present and the source gives a concrete outcome list.

Required data:

- co-present planets;
- sign/house;
- sect;
- dignity/host relation;
- visibility/under-rays;
- benefic/malefic testimony.

Procedure:

1. Treat co-presence as a combined testimony field.
2. Identify each planet's natural significations and current topic role.
3. Judge the condition of both planets rather than copying the listed outcome.
4. Check whether a benefic witness eases a difficult pair or a malefic witness damages a helpful pair.
5. Convert concrete lists into "what topic is being joined to what pressure/support."

Guardrail:

Do not import planet-pair cookbooks as final delineations.

Destination:

- `rules/significator-hierarchy.md`
- `rules/aspect-reception-rules.md`

Procedure:

1. Extract the active conditions: angularity, sect, dignity, phase, benefic/malefic contact.
2. Extract the topic domain from the place.

Guardrail:

No placement-by-placement reading in skill output.

## Rule Card II.12 — Host House / Bound Shows Domain Transfer

Source: `[Dorotheus *Carmen* II.32.1-4, II.33.1-5, II.34.1-4, II.35.1-4, II.36.1-4, II.37.1-15 via 'Umar al-Tabari → Dykes 2017, printed pp. 169-175]` `[new to Dorotheus]`

Use when:

- a planet is in another planet's domicile or bound and Dorotheus gives domain-specific outcomes.

Required data:

- tenant planet;
- host planet by domicile/bound;
- tenant condition;
- host condition;
- topic relevance of both planets.

Procedure:

1. Identify the host planet as the domain provider.
2. Identify the tenant planet as acting through the host's topics/resources.
3. Check whether tenant and host are benefic/malefic, dignified, afflicted, or supported.
4. Use this as a host-domain relation, not as an independent event recipe.

Guardrail:

Do not double-count a host relation as both reception and a separate testimony unless the chart supplies additional contact.

Destination:

- `rules/aspect-reception-rules.md`
- `rules/significator-hierarchy.md`

## Rule Card II.13 — Book II Topic Lots Should Remain Optional Modules

Source: `[Dorotheus *Carmen* II.2-II.6, II.10-II.11 via 'Umar al-Tabari → Dykes 2017, printed pp. 121-138]` `[new to Dorotheus]`

Use when:

- planning future Lot support in the skill.

Procedure:

1. Register each Lot with formula, topic, and source verse.
2. Require exact birth time and verified calculation.
3. Keep topic Lots below primary topic house, natural significator, and major Fortune/Spirit anchors unless repeated testimony supports them.
4. Add only the Lots needed for the user's question.

Guardrail:

Avoid Lot clutter. Dorotheus uses many Lots, but not every reading needs them.

Destination:

- `rules/lots-and-fortune.md`
