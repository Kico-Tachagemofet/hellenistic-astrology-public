# Brennan Method Targets

This file tracks which chapters should feed which functional rule files. It is not a book summary.

## Core Rule Modules

### `rules/significator-hierarchy.md`

Primary Brennan targets:

- Chapter 10: places as topic fields.
- Chapter 12: domicile lord of the hour-marker / Ascendant ruler.
- Chapter 13: rulers of the places.
- Chapter 16: Lots as derived significators.
- Chapter 17: annual profections as temporary focus setters.

Questions to extract:

- When is the Ascendant ruler the primary chart significator?
- How does a topic house become the focus?
- How does a house ruler carry that topic elsewhere?
- When should a Lot override or supplement a house/topic?
- How do time-lord techniques temporarily activate a house or planet?

### `rules/planet-condition-audit.md`

Primary Brennan targets:

- Chapter 7: planet significations, benefic/malefic, sect, visibility, speed, retrograde.
- Chapter 8: domicile, exaltation, adversity, triplicity, bounds, decans. Extracted to `ch08-signs-dignities.md` and deterministic tables in `rules/dignities-reference.md`.
- Chapter 10: angularity and good/bad places.
- Chapter 14: bonification and maltreatment.
- Chapter 15: triplicity rulers of the sect light.

Questions to extract:

- Does the planet have intrinsic capacity?
- Does it have accidental power to act?
- Is it helped, harmed, hidden, seen, overcoming, or enclosed?
- Is the planet constructive or problematic in this chart's sect?

### `rules/dignities-reference.md`

Primary Brennan targets:

- Chapter 8: domicile rulers and host logic.
- Chapter 8: exaltation/depression and adversity/exile.
- Chapter 8: standard Dorothean triplicity rulers by sect.
- Chapter 8: Egyptian bounds and Chaldean-order decans.

Questions to extract:

- Which planet has sign-level authority by domicile?
- Is the planet elevated, depressed/fallen, or in adversity/exile?
- Which triplicity ruler is primary in this chart's sect?
- Which bound and decan rulers apply when exact degree is known?
- Which dignity claims require exact degree data?
