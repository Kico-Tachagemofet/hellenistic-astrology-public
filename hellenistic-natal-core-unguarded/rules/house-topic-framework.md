# House Topic Framework

Purpose: turn houses into structured topic flows instead of isolated placement meanings.

## House System Policy

Use whole sign houses / whole sign places as the default Hellenistic topic skeleton.

```text
Rising sign = entire 1st place.
Next sign = 2nd place.
Continue in zodiacal order.
```

Record quadrant angles separately. The MC degree remains important as a public/action witness and angularity marker, especially when it falls outside the 10th whole sign place.

## Core Distinction

A planet in a house and a ruler of a house in another house are different evidence types.

```text
Planet in house = local tenant / actor inside that topic field.
Ruler of house = manager or carrier of that topic, showing where and how the topic is routed.
```

## Topic-Ruler Flow Formula

```text
Source house S -> ruler R -> destination house D
```

Read this as:

> The topic of S is carried into, enacted through, or entangled with the topic of D by the condition and behavior of R.

## Procedure

1. Define the chart focus and topic house.
2. Identify source house `S` by whole sign place.
3. Identify domicile ruler `R` of S.
4. Locate R by whole sign house and degree.
5. Audit R using `planet-condition-audit.md`.
6. Record planets in S as local tenants.
7. Record aspects/receptions to R and S.
8. Record angularity and quadrant MC involvement as strength/public-action witnesses.
9. Check whether the topic is activated by time lord or Lot.
10. Produce support, constraint, unknowns, and confidence.

## Evidence Types

| Evidence | Function | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| Topic house condition | local field | medium |
| Topic ruler condition | manager/carrier | high |
| Topic ruler destination | where topic is routed | high |
| Planets in topic house | local actors | medium-high |
| Natural significator | symbolic witness | medium |
| Lot or Lot ruler | derived topic witness | medium-high when relevant |
| Time lord activation | timing and salience | high for current period |
| Benefic/malefic testimony | support or pressure | high when close/relevant |
| Quadrant angle / MC contact | visibility, public action, manifestation | medium-high |

## Angularity And Place Strength

- Angular places are more capable of manifesting visibly.
- Succedent places can sustain, follow, accumulate, or support.
- Cadent/declining places are less direct and may show hidden, preparatory, declining, dissipating, or displaced action.
- Difficult places require mitigation checks before issuing strong negative claims.

## Difficult House Guardrail

For 6th, 8th, and 12th topics or placements:

```text
Do not conclude doom.
Do not prettify without evidence.
State pressure domain + mitigation + possible constructive channel.
```

## Good/Bad And Aversion Logic

Some places are difficult because they are in aversion to the Ascendant or belong to declining/dissipating triads. Treat this as environmental quality, not an automatic verdict.

If a difficult place has strong dignity, time-lord activation, benefic testimony, or a direct role in the chart focus, it can become highly important.

## Whole Sign vs Quadrant MC

Use whole sign houses for topic routing unless the chosen tradition/source says otherwise.

Also record the quadrant MC degree as a public/action witness, especially when it falls outside the 10th whole sign place.
