  / domain examples)

- Abstract typology ("works with others", "sometimes tension") —

- Single option / no alternatives offered
- No evidence cite / no "why" explanation
- Missing not-fit segment (reader cannot self-rule-out)
- No conditional dimension
- Over-assertion ("will X" rather than "may X")
